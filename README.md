U.S. Domestic Airline Pricing — Transparency & Fairness Policy Review
MSc Business Analytics · Hult International Business School · Spring 2026
BCG-style consulting engagement advising the U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) and Federal Aviation Administration (FAA)
Overview
Analysis of an 82M+ row flight pricing dataset in Snowflake to detect pricing anomalies, behavioural patterns, and transparency gaps in the U.S. domestic aviation market. Findings were translated into actionable regulatory policy recommendations.
Deliverables

Policy 1 – Historical Data Fare Disclosure (HDFA): Mandate a low-fare calendar and year-long historical pricing trends by route
Policy 2 – Fare Availability Disclosure Policy (FADP): Require OTAs to disclose real seat availability at each price point
Policy 3 – Airport Operating Cost Disclosure (AOCD): Require airlines to itemise airport-driven cost components in final fares

Tools & Technologies
Snowflake · SQL · Streamlit in Snowflake (SiS) · Python
